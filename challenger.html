You are a
Challenger !


Challengers have a penchant for control, seeking to exert it not only over their own lives but sometimes extending it to others as well. Strong-willed and assertive, they possess mental, spiritual, and emotional strength. Protective, resourceful, and straightforward, they are decisive individuals, though they may occasionally exhibit egocentric and domineering traits. Challenges they face include a need for control that can lead to confrontational and intimidating behavior, with tempers being a common issue. Allowing themselves to be vulnerable might be another hurdle to overcome.

At their best, Challengers master themselves and use their strength to uplift others, becoming heroic, magnanimous, and inspirational. They take charge of situations and seek control over their lives and those around them. As leaders, they pride themselves on their strength and resilience, motivating others to confront obstacles head-on and emerge victorious.
