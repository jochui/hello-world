You are a
Helper!

As the name suggests, Helpers embrace a nurturing and resourceful nature, always eager to lend assistance wherever needed. Empathetic, sincere, and warm-hearted, they exude genuine kindness. They are friendly, generous, and self-sacrificing but may resort to sentimentality, flattery, and people-pleasing behaviors. Challenges they face include unintentionally doing things for others merely to feel needed and difficulty acknowledging their own needs. Possessiveness may also arise as an issue.

At their best, Helpers exemplify unselfishness and altruism, offering unconditional love to others. They radiate a genuine kindness that nurtures and uplifts those in their presence, creating a nurturing environment where people feel cared for and valued.
